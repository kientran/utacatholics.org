Who We Are

Hello Mavricks!

Welcome to the University Catholic Community at UTA! We are so excited that you are checking us out and we hope to meet you soon! There are so many great things going on here at the UCC and we'd love to see you be a part of this awesome organization. Stop in any time to get involved, share your gifts, seek deeper meaning from your faith, serve others and worship with a prayerful heart.

We have a number of opportunities to involve each of you! Please check this web site often to find out what's new. We offer retreats, community service, fellowship activities, weekly prayers and the celebration of the Eucharist.

The Catholic Center is located right off of Cooper St. on Benge Drive -come and check it out! We have a GREAT building with a dedicated prayer chapel and our own worship space - and plenty of FREE parking! Come shoot a game of pool or a few hoops, use the study lounge (we have free wireless) or just hang out between classes and grab a snack. This ministry is here to help you grow spiritually and succeed academically. Please call on me anytime with your needs and concerns.

In Christ,
Stephanie Milligan, Director
